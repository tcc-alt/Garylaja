Mission & Vision

Christlikeness

Catching the Vision of Transformation to becoming a Christ-like Christian, we are committed to the Great Commandment and the Great Commission as a Conqueror.

Life Sharer

We are united in ensuring that every member of the Taytay Church of Christ joins the Life Group; therefore, strengthening the Life Group and equipping leaders is a top priority.

Goal Setter

Transformation happens in every area of our lives Spiritually, Physically, Mentally, Emotionally and Financially, thus every goal set has this purpose behind it.
